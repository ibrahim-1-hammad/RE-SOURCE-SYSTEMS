Action Plan & Task List

The project is divided into 4 phases. Select a phase below to view the detailed tasks required for completion. This interactive list helps you focus on one stage at a time.

1. Business & Legal

  • Finalize business name and register the domain name.
  • Obtain necessary business licenses and health/food regulatory permits.
  • Set up legal documents: Terms of Service, Privacy Policy, Shipping/Return Policy.

2. Branding

  • Design and finalize the Logo.
  • Select platform theme and apply the custom color palette and fonts.

3. Financial Setup

  • Set up business bank accounts.
  • Integrate and test payment gateways.

4. Platform Build

  • Install the chosen platform (Shopify/WooCommerce).
  • Install essential apps (SEO, reviews, security, subscription).

5. Product Sourcing & Inventory

  • Finalize supplier agreements and initial inventory.
  • Create master product list with exact nutritional data, ingredients, and expiration rules.

6. Photography & Content

  • Shoot high-quality professional photos for all products.
  • Write compelling, SEO-optimized product descriptions and category introductions.

7. Shipping Logistics

  • Define shipping zones, carriers, and rates.
  • Source and design custom packaging.

8. Quality Assurance (QA)

  • Test entire purchase flow: Cart, coupons, tax, shipping, payment.
  • Test account creation, login, and profile management.

9. Compliance Check

  • Verify all pages load correctly on Mobile, Tablet, and Desktop.
  • Check legal documents visibility and linking (especially at checkout).

10. Marketing Setup

  • Set up Google Analytics 4 (GA4) and Google Search Console.
  • Define initial SEO keywords.
  • Set up social media profiles (Instagram/TikTok).
  • Test Email Automations (Welcome sequence, Abandoned Cart).

11. Launch Day

  • Remove password protection/maintenance mode.
  • Submit sitemap to search engines.
  • Announce on social media and launch the first email campaign.

12. Post-Launch

  • Monitor Sales Funnel: Identify drop-off points.
  • Collect Reviews: Actively solicit customer reviews.
  • Inventory Management: Regularly update stock levels and monitor expiration dates.

Essential Page Structure

These core pages are the foundation of the user experience and are critical for building trust and driving conversions.

1. Homepage

High-impact banner, New Arrivals, Bestsellers, "Shop by Diet" blocks (Keto, Vegan), Trust badges.

2. Product Page (Crucial)

Must clearly display Nutritional Facts, Certifications (e.g., Organic), ingredients, and customer reviews.

3. About Us

Focus on your mission, sourcing commitment, quality standards, and transparency.

4. Blog / Resources

Content marketing hub. Example: "5 Easy Keto Dinner Recipes" or "Benefits of Ashwagandha".
